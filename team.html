The People Behind KRAShub

Built by patients,
for patients.

KRAShub.org is a labor of love by KRAS Kickers — a community of patients, caregivers, advocates, and clinicians united by a shared mission: make clinical trials understandable and accessible for everyone living with a KRAS-driven cancer. These are the people who started KRAS Kickers and those who are helping the mission.
