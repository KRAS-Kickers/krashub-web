Privacy First

Privacy Protection
by Design

We believe patients deserve to explore their options without being watched, matched, or monetized.

No tracking or sharing

We are not here to match you, so we don't track or share info about you.

Zero data collection

No searches, filters, locations, or cancer type interest are collected or tracked.

One-time email codes

Patients stay informed about when an email is used and where it goes. Each access uses a one-time code.
